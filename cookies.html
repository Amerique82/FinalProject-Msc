Cookie Policy

How this site uses cookies

Last updated: July 2026. This policy explains what cookies and similar technologies the Computing Department Hub uses, why, and how you can control them. For how we handle personal data more broadly, see the Privacy Notice.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. This Hub also uses local storage, a similar browser technology, to remember your preferences between visits. Neither can run programs or deliver viruses.

Cookies and storage we use

Cookies and local storage used by this site
NameTypePurposeDuration
rh-computing-theme Essential · local storage Remembers whether you chose light or dark mode. Until you clear your browser data
a11yBannerDismissed Essential · local storage Remembers that you dismissed the accessibility notice, so it is not shown again. Until you clear your browser data
Analytics Optional · cookie Anonymous usage statistics that help us understand which pages are useful. Up to 12 months

What we do not use

No advertising cookies, no third-party tracking cookies, and no profiling technologies are used anywhere on this site.

Managing cookies

Essential storage cannot be switched off, as the site would not remember your preferences without it. You can clear it at any time from your browser settings (usually under “Clear browsing data”), and most browsers let you block or delete cookies per site. To opt out of analytics, contact us below.
