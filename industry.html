Why Partner?

Mutual benefit, real impact

Our partnerships are built around shared goals — not just sponsorship. Here's what you gain.

Talent Pipeline

Access 320+ Computing students across AI, cybersecurity, data science, software engineering and web development — ready to contribute from day one.

Research Collaboration

Work with our active research groups on real-world problems. Joint publications, externally funded projects and shared IP are all on the table.

Brand Visibility

Feature in our student events, this portal, social channels and annual Industry Fair — staying front-of-mind throughout the academic year.

Community Impact

Shape what the next generation of developers and analysts learn. Help make the curriculum relevant, diverse and industry-ready.

Process

How it works

Getting started is simple. Most partnerships are formalised within 4–6 weeks.

1

Reach out

Send us a brief about your organisation and what kind of partnership interests you — placements, research, curriculum, job postings or events.

2

We match you

The department matches you with relevant programmes, research groups or student cohorts. We arrange an introductory meeting within 2 weeks.

3

Partner up

Sign a partnership agreement, get onboarded, and start contributing — whether that's a guest lecture, a sponsored hackathon challenge or a full placement programme.
