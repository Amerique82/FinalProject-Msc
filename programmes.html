Admissions

Entry requirements & how to apply

Everything you need to know before you apply — and the prospectus to take away.

Typical entry requirements

  • Undergraduate (BSc/BA): 96–120 UCAS points (e.g. CCC–BBB at A-level or equivalent). GCSE Maths and English at grade 4/C.
  • Foundation & HTQ: 32–64 UCAS points; relevant experience considered.
  • Postgraduate (MSc): a 2:2 honours degree or above. Conversion routes accept any discipline.
  • International: IELTS 6.0 overall (5.5 in each band) or equivalent.

Requirements vary by programme — always check the programme page before applying.

How to apply — step by step

  1. Choose your programme from the catalogue above.
  2. Check entry requirements and key dates on the programme page.
  3. Undergraduate: apply through UCAS (deadline: end of January for autumn entry).
  4. Postgraduate / short courses / apprenticeships: apply directly online.
  5. Receive your offer — most decisions arrive within 2–4 weeks.
