Privacy & Cookies

Privacy policy

Last updated: July 2026. This policy explains how the Computing Department Hub collects and uses information, in line with the UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018.

What we collect

We collect information you provide directly — such as your name and email address when you submit the contact or feedback forms — and limited technical data (browser type, pages visited) used to improve the Hub.

How we use it

Your information is used only to respond to enquiries, improve our services and meet our legal obligations as a public institution. We never sell personal data, and we share it only with university services required to resolve your enquiry.

Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting dataprotection@roehampton.ac.uk. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO).

Cookie policy

The Hub uses a small number of cookies and similar technologies:

  • Essential: remember your dark-mode preference and keep forms working. These cannot be switched off.
  • Analytics: anonymous usage statistics that help us understand which pages are useful. You can opt out below.

No advertising or third-party tracking cookies are used on this site.
